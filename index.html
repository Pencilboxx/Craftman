Some of the crafts

Mat(madur)

Parts of Bengal where madur is made
1. Bhagawanpur(Purba Medinipore)
2. Sabang(Paschim Medinipore)

Bell Metal(kansha)

Baharampore part of Murshidabad of Bengal makes the bell metal products.

Tant saree

Shantipur is the place where handloom sarees are made till date.
It is one of the comfortable most saree in this hot and humid country.

Masks

It is also known as mukhosh is Bengali. It is one of the most important ornament during Chou Nrityo made in purulia part of Bengal.

Conch curving(sankha)

Blowing Sankha is the part of hindu rituals during puja and this is made in Digha the coastal part of Bengal
